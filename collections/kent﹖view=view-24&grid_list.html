While there are many companies around the world that manufacture brushes, Kent has the distinction of being known as the world’s oldest company, having set up shop all the way back in 1777.

Since their original incorporation as Kent Brushes, the company has gone on to expand its product range to include combs, shaving soaps, shaving brushes and more. Today the company is moreover considered a lifestyle brand, having expanded to women and children’s markets.

When men purchase Kent brushes, combs or other products, they tend to commonly pair them with: Jack Dean Bay Rum Body Scrub Bar, Dapper Dan Pomade, Bluebeards Original Beard Wash and Captain Fawcett’s Beard Balm.

There is a lot of backstory to work with when marketing Kent’s products to your customers. However, if you feel like you could use some training on the best ways to curate your way to more sales, our team will be happy to train you (or your staff) at no extra cost.

About Perma Brands: we are North America’s leading distributor of premium wholesale Kent products and offer retailers (our most common customers are men’s clothing stores, tobacconists and barbershops) the widest variety of Kent brushes, combs and wholesale double edge razors.
