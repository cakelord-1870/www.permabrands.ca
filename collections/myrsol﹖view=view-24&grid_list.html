Located in the heart of Barcelona, Myrsol was founded in the early 1940s by Miquel Solé Ricou and has since established itself as a leader in the premium aftershave market.

Myrsol’s laboratory has diligently developed several lines of aftershaves for different facial hair and skin types including astrigents, emulsions and antesols. They’ve alos branched out into shaving creams.

When men place orders for Myrsol products, they very commonly place those orders with the following items from other brands: Jack Dean Pomade, Omega Shaving Brushes, Merkur Double Edge razors, Bolin Webb Face Balm and Captain Fawcett’s Beard Oil.

We’re very enthusiastic about the Myrsol brand and if you aren’t quite sure how you and your retail staff should market the product line to your customers, just let us know and our sales staff will be more than happy to lend their expertise and train you on the best way to curate Myrsol’s items.

About Perma Brands: we are North America’s leading distributor of premium wholesale Myrsol products and offer retailers (our most common customers are men’s clothing stores, tobacconists and barbershops) the widest variety of Myrsol aftershaves, shaving cream and wholesale double edge razors.

Please note - Perma Brands only sells wholesale shaving products to qualified retailers.
