When it comes to brand stories, you’d be hard-pressed to find a more intriguing one than Captain Fawcett’s which turns its line of shaving products into an elaborate adventure.

The fictitious Captain Peabody Fawcett has laid the groundwork for the company to craft various product lines that lend themselves to a more classic era of men’s grooming.

Over the past couple of decades, Captain Fawcett has gone on to release a large variety of mustache wax, beard oil, beard balm, shaving creams and alum bars.
