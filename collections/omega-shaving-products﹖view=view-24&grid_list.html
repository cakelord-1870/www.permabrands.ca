When you mention the name Omega to men’s hair care enthusiasts, the first thing to come to mind is shaving brushes. But did you know that Omega used to manufacture brush bristles...and not any actual brushes?

And when Omega did start producing brushes, they initially focused on paint brushes.

To this day Omega continues to serve the paint sector, even adding paint rollers to their brand line-up.

However, when it comes to premium shaving brushes, is the go-to choice for men wet shaving at home as well as for professional hairstylists and barbers.

Omega offers badger bristle brushes and synthetics.

Some of the best complimentary products for Omega shaving brushes includes: Alum Blocs by Osma to treat nicks and cuts, Jack Dean shaving soap or Dapper Dan Barbershop Classic Shaving Cream.
